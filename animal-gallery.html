My Favorite Animals

Butterflies

Butterflies are winged insects characterized by large, often brightly coloured wings that often fold together when at rest, and at a conspicuous, fluttering flight.

Birds

Birds are vertebrate animals adapted for flight. Many can also run, jump, swim, and dive.

Cats

Domestic cats are characterized by retractable claws, powerful bodies, acute senses, long tails, and specialized teeth adapted for hunting prey

Dolphins

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals and are part of the family of toothed whales that includes orcas and pilot whales.

Rabbits

Rabbits or bunnies are small mammals familiar throughout the world as a small herbivore, a prey animal, a domesticated form of livestock, and a pet, having a widespread effect on ecologies and cultures.

Elephants

Elephants are the largest living land animals. Three living species are currently recognized: the African bush elephant, the African forest elephant, and the Asian elephant.
